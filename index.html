How to register yourself?

Remember, registration does not mean you can walk in to a hospital and get vaccinated. You need to register first and if for your age group, walk in vaccination is not allowed, you will have to book slot first.

    How to get availability alerts?

    Since the vaccination for 18+ age group started, India is running short of vaccines. People who want to book are not able to find slots in the app.

    One tool users can try is a website called Under45.in created by developer Berty Thomas. Targeted particularly at users in the 18-44 age bracket, Under45 has a feature that will alert users on Telegram when a vaccination slot is available in a centre near them.

    Go to under45.in

    Enter your state and district and you will get a link to join the telegram channel of your area.

    Tips

    Tips to book an appointment slot

    • Subscribe to the telegram channel of your area.
    • Try booking through Aarogya Setu app as it does not ask you to fill a captcha.
    • Usually slots are made available during 7 PM to 10 PM.
    • Stay logged into Co-win, either through Co-win portal or Aarogya setu app.
    • The session times out after 10 mins. Keep re logging in & keep your session active.
    • As soon as you get a telegram alert, pick the pin code from that & do a search based on pincode.
    • Once booked do confirm that you have an appointment slip.

    Things to note when going for vacination

    • Wear a double mask.
    • Reach the hospital 10-15 minutes before your slot.
    • Maintain social distancing.
    • Keep the ID proof that you used for registration & the appintment slip, even soft copy is fine.
    • Keep cash handy if you have booked a paid slot, many hospitals are accepting payment by cash only.
    • Keep a pen with you as you may have to fill hospital's own registration or patient declaration form.
    • If you face any side effect, do not panic. Usually side effects of vaccination go away in a day or two but if they persist do consult a doctor.
